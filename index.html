Message from the Conference President
MESSAGE FROM THE CONFERENCE PRESIDENT

I bring you greetings on behalf of Western North Ghana Conference. We are grateful to God for the opportunity to meet for this year’s Annual Camp Meeting and we cherish this unique opportunity for our members to gather together to worship, fellowship and rekindle friendships. The theme for this year’s Camp Meeting is ‘‘I WILL GO: CHOSEN FOR MISSION’’.

Why I will go: chosen for mission as a theme? “Seventh-day Adventists have been chosen by God as a peculiar people, separate from the world. He has made them His representatives and has called them to be ambassadors for Him in the last work of salvation. The greatest wealth of truth ever entrusted to mortals, the most solemn and fearful warnings ever sent by God to man, have been committed to them to be given to the world.” (Testimonies for the Church, vol. 7, p. 138.) In WNGC every church member is to give 2 hours each week for evangelism and visitation.

As we go through the activities for this year’s camp meeting, I humbly want to remind all members that, we are the watchmen and light bearers of the world. We have been given a work of the most solemn import, the proclamation of the first, second, and third angels’ messages. We are to allow nothing else to absorb our attention and must be ready to share our faith. God has chosen us for His mission as He equips us with all the necessary power.

In this spiritual gathering, I would ask you to consider three things:

  • To love kindness, and to walk humbly with the Lord
  • Seeking the Power of the Holy Spirit to live above sin
  • Expecting the Lord to save us from the burdens of this world as we pray

Through inspirational preaching and in-depth Bible studies, joyful singing, and warm-hearted fellowship, you will certainly encounter the God of Abraham during this 6-days meeting. I wish you the blessings of the fruits of the spirit - love, joy, peace, patience, kindness, generosity, faithfulness, gentleness, and self-control. There will be joy when the work is done.

Maranatha!!!

Pastor Joseph Ackah-Miezah

President,

Western North Ghana Conference of Seventh-day Adventist Church
